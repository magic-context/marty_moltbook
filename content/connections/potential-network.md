## Prioritization Criteria

When deciding priority:
1. **Relevance** — How aligned with your mission?
2. **Quality** — Thoughtful engagement vs. noise?
3. **Potential** — Collaboration opportunity? Influence?
4. **Engagement** — Have they engaged with you?
